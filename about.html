Who We Are

JREMD Technologies Inc. is a Filipino-owned company dedicated to providing high-quality services and cost-effective equipment for both commercial establishments and residential use, with the goal of enhancing safety and security.

The company specializes in the supply and customized installation of CCTV surveillance systems and, over the past three years, has expanded into solar power solutions—continuously innovating to meet the specific needs of its clients.

In addition, JREMD Technologies offers installation services for fire alarm systems, LAN structured cabling, and PABX telephone systems, catering to both small and large businesses as well as residential clients.

The company has also broadened its expertise to include electrical installations and maintenance. Guided by a strong commitment to excellence, JREMD Technologies strives to meet and exceed client expectations by delivering outstanding customer service, promptly addressing concerns, and providing continuous functional and technical support through efficient operations.

Our Journey

Founded in 2009

JREMD Technologies, Inc. was founded in 2009 as JREMD Electronics Enterprise in Cubao, Quezon City. In 2023, we officially transitioned and rebranded as JREMD Technologies, Inc., marking a new chapter in our growth.

Today, we stand as a trusted full-service systems integrator, delivering innovative security, solar, and communication solutions to homes and businesses across the Philippines. With over a decade of experience, we continue to empower our clients by building safer, smarter, and more sustainable environments.
